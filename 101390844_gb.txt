George Brown College is one of the most important Colleges in Toronto.
Around 30,000 students attend george brown college per year.
It has 3 campuses in the GTA whcih are :
St. James Campus: This is the largest and main campus of George Brown College.
It is located in downtown Toronto and offers a wide range of programs.
Waterfront Campus: located by Lake Ontario, this campus focuses on programs related to business,
hospitality, health sciences, and photopgraph .
Casa Loma Campus: This campus is known for its historic architecture and offers programs in arts, 
design, and computer science.
Toronto Matropoltin University Partnership: George Brown College collaborates with TMU at their joint TMU George Brown Campus.
